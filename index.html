Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur

I am a final year undergraduate in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, IIT Kanpur. I am genuinely interested in maths, with statistics being my newfound passion. Recently, I’ve been working on Bayesian statistics, and its power to simplify problems significantly continues to amaze me (given you have the right belief!). I am currently working with Prof. Dootika Vats on output analysis of multiple Markov chains.

I’ve also worked extensively on deep learning in college from a research as well as industrial perspective. My recent experiences on developing trading strategies for a quantitative trading firm introduced me to the power and wide applicability of statistics in practical problems. I am always looking for opportunities to work on interesting and challenging problems to keep my brain churning.
